driver, experience 15 years

Hello! We've been cooperating for a little over a year and I'm really happy with my work. First, I thought that it would be the usual additional earnings after work, no more. But after three months I realized that here I earn more than at my main job. We had problems with our bosses, but here we have a normal peer-to-peer attitude. And I left there - here, and I do not regret it! This is not a part-time job, but a full-fledged job. The contract was signed officially, I work and earn money. I think I made the right choice.

Here is a good approach and, above all, respect for us. And I am always calm about getting the money I have earned.
